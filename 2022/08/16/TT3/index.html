Created2022-08-16Updated2022-08-18Visited

Greedy algorithm, for lack of a better word, is good. Greedy algorithm is right, greedy algorithm works. Greedy algorithm clarifies, cuts through, and captures the essence of the evolutionary spirit. Greedy algorithm, in all of its forms; greedy for life, for money, for love, knowledge has marked the upward surge of mankind.

String Reconstruction

Credit Card

显然，在只需在检查的时候存钱。所以维护每次存的上界与下界，就可以在不超过 ddd 的前提下尽可能减少存钱的次数。
